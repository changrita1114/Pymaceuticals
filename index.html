Summary: Total Tumor Volume vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analyze how total tumor volumes change in ten different drug regimens. To accomplish this analysis, data was pulled from the two different tables to assemble a dataset of 249 mice and over 1800 total tumor volume measurement points.After assembling the dataset, Matplotlib was used to visualize the results and the statistical analysis was compeleted using SciPy. The five top regimens were ranked using mean of the total tumor volumes at the last measurement point. The five regimens were Ramicane, Capomulin, Propriva, Ceftamin and Infubinol. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, as well as explantions and descriptions of any trends and correlations witnessed.
